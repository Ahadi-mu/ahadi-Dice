Our Location

Find JOY Hotel in the heart of the city, surrounded by top attractions and convenient transport links.

By Air

International Airport is just 25 minutes away. Private transfers available upon request. Taxi and ride-share services readily available at the terminal.

By Train

Central Station is within walking distance (5 minutes). Direct connections to all major cities. Hotel concierge can arrange pickup service.

By Car

Located on Luxury Avenue, 2 blocks from Main Street. Secure parking available. Electric vehicle charging stations on-site.
