Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about JOY Hotel.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is available from 3:00 PM onwards, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged upon request, subject to availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast is included in certain packages and promotional rates. Please check your booking details. Breakfast can also be added during the booking process for an additional fee.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services. Please contact our concierge at least 24 hours in advance to arrange pickup. Charges apply.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before check-in for standard bookings. After that, the first night is non-refundable. Special non-refundable rates cannot be cancelled.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel! Well-behaved dogs and cats are welcome in designated rooms for a fee of $50 per night. Maximum 2 pets per room.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer on-site parking for guests. The parking fee is $25 per night. EV charging stations are also available.

What amenities are included?

All rooms include: Free WiFi, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, mini-bar, safe, coffee/tea maker, hairdryer, and 24-hour room service.

Do you have a gym and spa?

Yes, we have a fully equipped fitness center and spa. The gym is open 24/7, and spa treatments are available by appointment.

Can I host events or meetings at the hotel?

Yes, we have multiple event spaces including a Grand Ballroom and meeting rooms. Contact events@joyhotel.com for inquiries.

What is the minimum age to check in?

Guests must be 18 years or older to check in. All guests must show a valid photo ID at check-in.

Is the hotel wheelchair accessible?

Yes, we have accessible rooms and facilities. Please inform us in advance if you require accessible amenities.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Cash payments are also accepted with a valid credit card imprint.
