Hotel Policies

Please review our policies to ensure a comfortable stay.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in: 3:00 PM onwards

Check-out: 11:00 AM

Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request (subject to availability). Please contact the front desk.

Cancellation Policy

Standard Booking: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. After that, the first night is non-refundable.

Non-refundable Rate: Full payment required at time of booking. No refunds for cancellations or no-shows.

Group Bookings: Different terms may apply. Please contact events@joyhotel.com.

Payment Policy

We accept all major credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

A valid credit card is required at check-in for security deposit. The deposit will be released upon check-out, minus any incidental charges.

Corporate accounts and third-party payments require prior approval. Contact reservations@joyhotel.com.

Pet Policy

We welcome well-behaved pets (dogs and cats) in designated pet-friendly rooms.

  • Maximum 2 pets per room
  • Pet fee: $50 per night
  • Pets must be supervised at all times
  • Pets are not allowed in dining areas or pool

Smoking Policy

JOY Hotel is a smoke-free property. Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas, including guest rooms.

Designated outdoor smoking areas are available. A cleaning fee of $250 will be applied for violations.

Age Policy

Guests must be 18 years or older to check in. Minors under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

Valid photo ID is required at check-in for all guests.

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. Please respect other guests and keep noise to a minimum during these hours.

Liability

The hotel is not responsible for any loss or damage to personal property. Guest rooms are equipped with safes for valuables.

Guests are advised to utilize the in-room safe and to keep their room doors locked.
